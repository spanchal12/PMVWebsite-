Why buy it?

Organize your vacation like a pro! No longer do you need to struggle to keep your vacation plans organized and it one place. Plan My Vacay is an easy to use application dedicated to making sure that all of your travel plans are stronged in one easy to reach spot. No more searching through emails to find dates, times, or locations for excursions you've reserved. With our clean user interface, users are able to locate important information about each aspect of their trip. In addition, we have a special checklist feature to make sure that you have packed everything you need! We offer user scheduled Notifications. Not to mention that all of the trip data is storing localing, ensure that it doesn't get sold companies. No more annoying ads to getting in the way of you and your travel plans. And of course, this app works 100% without an internet connection so you do not have to worry about getting stranded without your plans due to the lack of connection.
